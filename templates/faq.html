Payment & Billing

How do I update my payment method?

You can update your payment method by going to Account Settings > Billing > Payment Methods. Click "Add New Method" or "Edit" next to your existing method.

Why was my payment declined?

Payment declines can happen due to insufficient funds, expired cards, or bank security measures. Please check with your bank or try a different payment method.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds can be requested within 30 days of purchase. Please contact our billing team with your order number and reason for refund.

Technical Issues

The application is running slowly

Try clearing your browser cache, checking your internet connection, and closing other browser tabs. If the issue persists, contact support.

I'm getting error messages

Please note the exact error message and the steps you took before it appeared. Contact support with this information for faster resolution.

Features not working properly

Ensure your browser is up to date and JavaScript is enabled. Try refreshing the page or using an incognito/private browsing window.

Account & Login

I forgot my password

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I change my email address?

Go to Account Settings > Profile and update your email address. You'll need to verify the new email before it becomes active.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account from Account Settings > Privacy. Please note this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Product Features

How do I upload files?

You can upload files by clicking the attachment button in the chat or dragging and dropping files directly into the chat area. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF (max 10MB).

What file types are supported?

We support image files: JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, BMP, and WebP. Maximum file size is 10MB per file.

How do I track my support tickets?

After creating a ticket, you'll receive a ticket ID. You can use this ID to check the status and add updates to your ticket.
